True Lebanese Food

The Lebanese are known for their unique take on traditional Arabic dishes. Our recipes were developed using high quality herbs and spices to create the freshest, most robust flavor possible. Our salads are bright and lemony and pair perfectly with heavier dishes. Our falafel recipe has been perfected over the years to be bursting with flavor, crisp on the outside and never, ever dry. Our meats are marinated and grilled to order to guarantee freshness.

A little bit about us

Owner, Abdallah Rajeh, grew up in the mountains of Lebanon during the civil war as the only son with 6 sisters. When he moved to Austin to attend school at UT, he began calling his mother asking for step-by-step instruction on how to cook the home cooked foods he was missing. It didn't take long before word got out and he found himself surrounded by hungry friends who were either homesick for traditional foods they couldn't find elsewhere, or others curious to experience Lebanese food and culture.

He discovered not only a passion for cooking, but the inexplicable joy that came from the community that developed around him. He spent years working in restaurants learning the ins and outs of business before opening something of his own. His drive has always been to have a place where people of all backgrounds can gather together to experience true Lebanese food. Abdallah is known for his larger than life presence, his gregarious personality, unmatched work ethic and deep love for his friends and family.
